Our Mission

Empowering Communities to End Hunger

NiHao Food Bank Initiative is a volunteer-driven grassroots movement focused on raising awareness about hunger in the USA, improving community engagement, and directing resources toward the North Texas Food Bank (NTFB) to fight hunger.

Our initiative galvanizes and mobilizes the Chinese diaspora by:

  • Increasing hunger awareness
  • Organizing volunteers
  • Conducting food drives
  • Raising crucial funds

Led by passionate supporters and volunteers, the NiHao movement operates in a non-political, non-religious ecosystem that puts the cause ahead of individual egos.

Solutions & Achievements

Our Key Initiatives

Through organized programs and community engagement, we translate goodwill into tangible meals for our neighbors.

Monthly Packing Day

Large-scale meal-packing events at NTFB's Perot Family Campus producing tens of thousands of meals monthly.

Peanut Butter Drive

In 2025, raised over $64,000 and 8,000 lbs of peanut butter from 70+ collection locations.

RMA/NiHao Charity Concert

Annual cultural fundraising event raising over $40,000 in both 2024 and 2025.
