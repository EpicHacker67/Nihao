Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I need to be to volunteer?

Youth ages 8 and up can participate in packing days with adult supervision. Youth ages 12-18 can join our Youth Ambassador Program for more leadership opportunities. Adults of all ages are welcome!

How often are packing days held?

We hold packing days every month at the North Texas Food Bank warehouse. The specific date varies, so check our events calendar or sign up for our newsletter to get notified.

Can I bring a group or team?

Absolutely! We welcome corporate teams, school groups, scout troops, and community organizations. Contact us to arrange a group volunteer session.

How do I get community service hours?

We provide verification of volunteer hours for all participants. After each event, you'll receive documentation that can be used for school requirements, college applications, or other purposes.

Where do donations go?

All donations to NiHao Food Bank Initiative go directly to supporting hunger relief efforts through the North Texas Food Bank. NTFB is one of the largest and most efficient food banks in the country, with every $1 providing approximately 3 meals.

How can my business become a partner?

We offer various partnership opportunities including sponsorships, corporate volunteer days, food drives, and event co-hosting. Contact us to discuss how we can work together to support our community.
