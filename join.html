Join & Pledge

Your decision to join NiHao, whether as an individual, family, student, or corporate partner, directly fuels the movement to end hunger in North Texas. Thank you for stepping up to make a powerful impact.

Join the Movement

Become a NiHao Ambassador

Target Audience: Students (Grades 6-12), Adults, and Families

Thank you for considering becoming an ambassador of the NiHao Food Bank Initiative! By joining us, you agree to make a philanthropic commitment and join us in raising money for the North Texas Food Bank to provide for hungry children all across North Texas.

The Initial Pledge

$300

To ensure every Ambassador starts with a tangible commitment, students are required to make a $300 initial pledge directly to the North Texas Food Bank.

How to Join

Ambassador Registration Steps

1

Fill in the Credentials

Fill in the credentials on the pledge form carefully.

2

Complete the Donation

Go to the North Texas Food Bank website and complete the donation of $300 on the donation bar. Take a screenshot of the receipt.

3

Email Documents

Scan the completed pledge form and email it with your donation screenshot to yangchen2006@gmail.com AND nihaofoodbank@gmail.com

4

Submit Membership Form

Fill out and submit the membership form.

All finished? Please be patient for the next couple of days waiting for an approval or response. Thank you again for joining us to support child hunger in North Texas with NiHao.

Corporate Partners

Corporate Partnership

We invite corporations and organizations to make a sustainable impact through a three-year pledge to one of our Sponsorship Circles. Your partnership is a powerful force in the fight against hunger.

Sponsorship Levels and Impact

Level Donation (Over 3 Years) Meals Enabled Details
Business Partner $50,000 150,000 meals Business Partner members stand among our strongest advocates in the fight against hunger. They raise hunger awareness through speaking engagements, meet with community groups, and seek out opportunities to represent NiHao in North Texas.
Platinum $20,000 60,000 meals Platinum members are the pillars of NiHao. These members ensure that the goals of the council are up-to-date and align with the overall mission to combat hunger.
Gold $10,000 30,000 meals Gold members rally behind the cause of fighting hunger and enable putting nutritious food on the table for hungry North Texans.
Silver $3,000 9,000 meals Silver members are the building blocks of the NiHao movement. They make up the bulk of NiHao's contributions and volunteers.
