About Us

Discover our mission, our story, and the passionate community behind NiHao Food Bank Initiative.

Our Mission

Fighting Hunger Through Community

NiHao Food Bank Initiative was founded to mobilize the Chinese American community and beyond to address food insecurity in North Texas. Through our partnership with the North Texas Food Bank, we've created meaningful opportunities for families, youth, and community members to make a tangible difference.

Our name, "NiHao" (你好), means "hello" in Chinese - a greeting that represents our welcoming, community-first approach. We believe that fighting hunger is about more than providing food; it's about building connections, empowering youth, and creating a culture of giving back.

Our core values:

  • • Community-Centric: We believe in neighbor helping neighbor
  • • Youth Empowerment: Developing the next generation of leaders
  • • Cultural Connection: Celebrating our heritage while serving all
  • • Partnership: Working together with NTFB and community organizations

Our Primary Partner

North Texas Food Bank

The North Texas Food Bank is our core partner in fighting hunger. As one of the largest food banks in the United States, NTFB provides access to over 125 million meals annually across 13 North Texas counties. Our partnership enables us to make a direct, measurable impact in our community.
