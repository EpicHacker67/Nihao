Monthly Packing Day

Large-scale meal-packing events at NTFB's Perot Family Campus

Our volunteers gather monthly at the North Texas Food Bank's (NTFB) Perot Family Campus in Plano. These are large-scale meal-packing events that produce tens of thousands of meals, turning volunteer hours into direct impact for hungry families.

What to expect:

  • 2-3 hour volunteer shifts
  • Family-friendly (ages 8+)
  • No experience needed
  • Great for groups and teams

Peanut Butter Drive

A signature, high-impact annual campaign during Hunger Action Month

A signature, high-impact campaign that has become one of our most successful annual fundraisers to support North Texas Food Bank. Peanut butter is kid-friendly, nutritious, and high in protein, but is often considered expensive and therefore not a popular donated item.

This drive runs during Hunger Action Month (September) and generates both food and financial contributions.

In 2025, NiHao raised over $64,000 and 8,000 lbs of peanut butter from over 70 collection locations.

NiHao Community Fundraising Card

Leveraging everyday commerce for philanthropy

The NiHao Community Fundraising Card is a key annual fundraising initiative that leverages everyday commerce for philanthropy. The program empowers community members to support local businesses while generating significant financial contributions for the North Texas Food Bank (NTFB).

The FY2026 campaign saw NiHao Ambassadors mobilize 2,000 local Chinese families to raise an impressive $40,000 in just two weeks.

Benefits include:

  • Support local businesses
  • Generate funds for NTFB
  • Easy way to give back
  • Special discounts from partner merchants

RMA/NiHao Charity Concert

Annual cultural fundraising event with Royal Music Academy

Our annual cultural fundraising event with Royal Music Academy utilizes the power of the arts to fight hunger. The concert features performances by renowned musicians and ensembles, celebrating both Eastern and Western musical traditions.

These concerts have made a significant impact, raising over $40,000 in both 2024 and 2025.

Highlights include:

  • Classical and contemporary performances
  • VIP reception with community leaders
  • Youth performer showcases
  • Silent auction and fundraising

NiHao Civic Engagement Program

Educating and empowering our community to advocate for solutions

Led by North Texas Food Bank and Feeding Texas, this program is designed to educate and empower our community, particularly our Youth Ambassadors, to advocate for policies and solutions that address the root causes of food insecurity in our region.

Our civic engagement initiatives include:

  • Connecting with elected officials on hunger relief policies
  • Voter registration and civic education
  • Community leadership development
  • Partnership with advocacy organizations

Dragon Boat Races

Celebrating culture while amplifying our hunger relief mission

NiHao participates in major cultural events like Dragon Boat Races, Lantern Festivals, and Lunar New Year celebrations. These vibrant gatherings are used to both celebrate culture and amplify our mission of hunger relief.

These events bring together students, families, and community groups to raise significant funds and spread awareness about food insecurity in our region.
