Peanut Butter Drive

Collecting protein-rich nutrition for families in need

Peanut butter is one of the most requested items at food banks - it's shelf-stable, protein-rich, and loved by kids. Our annual Peanut Butter Drive mobilizes the community to collect thousands of jars for North Texas families.
