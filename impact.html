Our Journey

Key Milestones

NiHao Food Bank Initiative Founded

A group of passionate community members came together with a vision to mobilize the Chinese American community for hunger relief.

First Packing Day

Our inaugural volunteer event at the North Texas Food Bank brought together 50 volunteers who packed over 10,000 meals.

Youth Ambassador Program Launched

Recognizing the passion of young volunteers, we created a dedicated program to develop youth leadership.

First Annual Charity Concert

A sold-out event featuring local musicians raised significant funds and awareness for hunger relief.

1 Million Meals Milestone

Through cumulative fundraising and volunteer efforts, we enabled our first million meals for North Texas families.

500+ Youth Ambassadors

Our Youth Ambassador Program grew to include over 500 active young volunteers.

Community Partner Network Expands

Formalized partnerships with 10+ community organizations to amplify our collective impact.

2.6 Million Meals Enabled

A major milestone celebrating the cumulative impact of our community's generosity.

Continuing to Grow

With your help, we're expanding our programs, reaching more neighbors, and developing more youth leaders than ever before.
