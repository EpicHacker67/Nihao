Special Initiative

NiHao "Food Truck"

The NiHao "Food Truck" is a branded mobile food distribution truck operating in Collin County, allowing NiHao to serve even more families directly in their communities. Funded by NiHao and supporting partners, this vehicle brings nutrition and hope to neighborhoods in need.

This initiative represents our commitment to expanding beyond warehouse operations and meeting families where they are.
