About the Program

What is the Youth Ambassador Program?

The NiHao Youth Ambassador Program is designed for young leaders ages 12-18 who want to make a difference in their community while developing valuable life skills.

As a Youth Ambassador, you'll participate in hands-on volunteer opportunities, leadership training sessions, and community events throughout the year. You'll join a supportive community of peers who share your passion for service.

Fill out the Youth Ambassador application form with your information and interests.
